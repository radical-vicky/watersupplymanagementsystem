FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the smart meter work?

Our IoT-enabled smart meters automatically record water consumption in real-time and transmit data securely to our cloud platform for monitoring and billing.

Can I pay via M-Pesa?

Yes! Water Supply Management System integrates directly with M-Pesa. You can pay your water bills instantly using Lipa Na M-Pesa or M-Pesa Paybill.

How accurate is the leak detection?

Our leak detection algorithms have a 95% accuracy rate, identifying abnormal usage patterns and alerting you before significant water loss occurs.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, secure data centers, and comply with Kenyan data protection regulations.
